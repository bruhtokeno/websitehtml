Our Mission

Bruhcrypto Finance is a Decentralized Exchange and NFT
marketplace focused on expanding MemeFi and helping people
in poverty.

Decentralized Exchange

Our DEX is a forked version of Uniswap, a market leader in
Decentralized Finance. Combining their aspects with ours,
the exchange is both user friendly and safe to use.

NFT Marketplace

Our NFT marketplace focuses on helping children in poverty
by letting them curate NFTs with low miner fees and sell
them for proceeds that they can spend on everyday goods.
