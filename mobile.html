  We're not a run-of-the-mill MemeFi project. We're creating a better
    future with web3, NFTs, erc-20, and DeFi.

Our Mission

  Bruhcrypto Finance is a Decentralized Exchange and NFT
  marketplace focused on expanding MemeFi and helping people
  in poverty by raising awareness about our mission and linking
  an NFT marketplace that features art made by people from
  impoverished areas with the Decentralized Exchange for
  users to easily swap and manage funds.

Developmental Progress

  At the moment, we are curently developing the landing page
  of the DEX and have laid plans for the NFT marketplace that
  will operate on either Solana or Ethereum with added code from
  Blockworks to limit the amount of miner fees involved in creating
  an NFT. Our DEX is also a forked version of Uniswap v2 and is
  almost ready to be released for beta testing with its backend
  contracts deployed on Rinkeby Testnet and frontend code currently
  being developed.

Competitive Landscape

  DeFi is an ever-expanding emerging market with stiff competiton
  and unique products. Some factors that make us stand out from our
  competitors such as Shiba Inu, Shibnobi, and Kishu Inu include,
  but are not limited to:

  1. A built-in NFT marketplace
  2. Planned Anti-bot code
  3. Being built on top of Uniswap
  4. Designed to help people in poverty
  5. A multichain focus in the DEX and NFT Marketplace

  (DEX is an acronym for Decentralized Exchange)
